Shree Shyam Satang Mandal (Reg. Mumbai) is a registered non profit organisation set up in YEAR to promote the welfare of people and the devotion towards Lord Shyam of Khatu. Every Year based on the donations recieved from its patrons, the mandal runs a Dharam Shala, THe Satsang Bhavan,KhatuShyamji, Carries out various public welfare initiatives like a monthly medical camp, food distribution and a 10 day medical cammp during the month of halgun. The mandal also organises kirtans in mumbai as per the needs of the bhakts. The mandal organises one major kirtan The Shyam Varshik Mahotsav, in the month of december which is open to all.
